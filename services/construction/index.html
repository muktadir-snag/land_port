ABP offers unparalleled experience in safely handling a wide range of cargoes for the construction industry. Cementitious, mineral or sensitive construction cargoes are routinely handled with precision and efficiency by our expert teams.

This is further supported by ongoing investment that ensures we offer the best possible facilities and equipment tailored to meet our customers’ needs.

From the lightest to the heaviest and from the awkward to the simple, ABP has the right facilities and expertise for all your cargo handling needs.

Delivering for our customers

ABP has recently invested more than £500 million in port facilities and equipment. This includes state-of-the-art cranes, terminal facilities, handling equipment and industry-leading bulk storage bays. ABP can discharge or load vessels faster than ever before, keeping cargoes safe and secure.

Further recent investments include specialist silo and covered storage, developed on behalf of customers, many benefitting from rail connectivity.
As well as a comprehensive range of ports around Britain, ABP’s Hams Hall rail terminal in the West Midlands is capable of handling a wide range of construction materials.

Utilising our port locations, we are well positioned to offer a multi-port distribution package with one consolidated contractual agreement, offering significant benefits for your business.

If you would like to find out more about how ABP can help your business please get in touch with our sector expert, Paul Litten.

Connectivity

As the UK’s largest port operator with a network of 21 ports around Britain, we offer excellent marine access. Our ports offer efficient road and rail links, providing swift access to destinations throughout the country. As the UK’s best-connected port operator, ABP provides an unparalleled range of locations, routes and logistics solutions ready to serve our customers’ business needs.

Furthermore, the vast majority of our port locations are adjacent to Crown Estate licenced areas for marine sand and gravel, with space for storage and onward distribution.
